Delete Your BodyOS Account

⚠️ Important

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data including nutrition logs, body measurements, and progress history will be permanently removed.

You can permanently delete your BodyOS account and all associated data directly from within the app:

  1. Open the BodyOS app on your device
  2. Navigate to Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  3. Review the deletion warning and confirm your decision
  4. Your account and all associated data will be permanently removed within 24 hours

What Gets Deleted

  • Your user account and profile information
  • All nutrition logs and food photos
  • Body measurements and weight history
  • Fasting logs and health data
  • App preferences and settings
  • Any subscription information (billing handled by app stores)
